The Literary Fest

Verba Maximus, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus’s pride and soul, is the national annual literary fest conducted every year. The words, ‘Verba Maximus’, are Latin for “Words of the Greatest” and right from the pro-shows and events to the competitors and organisers, everything lives up to this title. We have conducted five successful editions of this fest, and the sixth edition – Verba Maximus ’17 – is much awaited! On February 4th and 5th, Verba Maximus returns to present to you a 2-day literary whirlwind that’s hard to miss!

To know more about the event please get in touch.

Why Verba Maximus?

Allow us to turn the clock backward in time in February, and take you on a journey like never before. The organizers of Verba Maximus '17 have arranged for a wide variety of events and Pro-Shows which are bound have to participants amazed and to give all an experience they will cherish forever. On February 4th and 5th, let's take a peek at "Timeless Tales" woven into an array of exhilarating events, only at VM! Verba Maximus takes prides in fostering literary culture among the diverse audience that it plays host to. Whether you're a writer, or a speaker, whether it's traditional debate that you prefer, or innovative events like 'Sherlocked', whether you're a Grammar Nazi or just discovering your love for language, there's guaranteed to be something for everyone here!

Kenneth Sebastian

Affectionately known as ‘Kenny’, Kenneth Sebastian has been a rising star on the Indian stand-up comedy scene. Taking inspiration from his middle-class background, most of his comedy centers on topics of culture, people, and stereotypes which has enabled him to reach the top of the comedy world in no time.

He's performed in over 500 shows in the last five years and among his various achievements, has also written and hosted the show 'The Living Room' on Comedy Central. So come down to BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus on February 6th, to be a part of yet another fun-packed laughter extravaganza by our very own Kenneth Sebastian, the closing act to the literary fest, Verba Maximus.

Ashwin Sanghi

One of the most popular English language fiction writers in India, Ashwin Sanghi has been a trailblazer in the genre of crime thrillers. A beloved author, Ashwin Sanghi's bibiliography is extensive and includes bestseller titles like "Chanakya's Chant", "The Krishna Key", and a murder mystery co-authored with James Patterson.

Come join us on the 4th of February for a talk presented by none other than Mr. Sanghi himself!

Indu Balachandran

The Author of of 'Runaway Writers' and a writer of humor columns and short story anthologies, this is one lady who's had a wide variety of experience. A travel writer with a sense of humor and a penchant for the unexpected, Ms Indu Balachandran is certain to light up whenever she goes with her gift. Verba Maximus is excited to be hosting Ms Balachandran onthe 5th of February.
