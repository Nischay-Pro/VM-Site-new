Feeling the thrill of the winter chill? November travel tip

As winter draws in on the Northern hemisphere, it’s got us thinking about all things Antarctica. November is one of the best times to travel on one of our Antarctica holidays to actually see penguins and photograph them on the snow. Travelling later in the season means the ice has melted enough to expose the muddy terrain underneath, which doesn’t make for the best wildlife or landscape photography.

If you want to travel to Antarctica in 2017, get in touch with us now to secure your spot at the right time for the best wildlife experience, and the ultimate landscapes, peppered with glistening icebergs under the 24 hour sun.
