With the development of the ‘world-wide web’ there are plenty of ‘specialist tour operators’ out there these days, so I don’t blame you in thinking ‘why will these guys be any different…?’…but hopefully what I can do is explain why we are different.

Firstly, we are not a tour operator. We are what we would like to call more of a ‘travel outfitter’. Our ‘selling people’ are not ‘sales people’, they are ‘destination specialists’. We don’t do a ‘bit of everything’, we specialist purely in the natural world and all that it has to offer. No villa holidays here, no trips to Egypt, no chalets and no visits to ancient ruins I am afraid. We specialise in putting you in the right place at the right time to truly maximise your wildlife safari experience. 

We are the pioneers of wildlife travel and we have the experience to show it.

Over 10 years ago now, I set up what most people thought was a bizarre little company (and a weird idea!) called World Primate Safaris…you guessed it…it specialised in safaris…to see primates! This niche enterprise was born from a passion for primates over the years of guiding in Africa – working in Gabon, Congo, CAR, Uganda & Rwanda, Madagascar, Cameroon and more! – working in the development world, and studying French and African and Asian Studies at University. 

From here, we decided to develop our niche expertise but keep our focus on the unique wildlife of the world with the result being the introduction of, World Big Cat Safaris and even World Bear Safaris! Personally I thought we had sold our souls and were no longer the niche experts we once were, it soon became apparent though, that you couldn’t get more niche than this, we didn’t specialise in countries like everyone else, we specialised wholeheartedly and precisely, in individual species and the wildlife itself. No other company had taken this stance, and we have forever been rewarded for such a unique approach to this very day.

We are not tour operators, we are not generalists, when it comes to wildlife, we are here to help.


For simplicities sake, we now go by the name of Natural World Safaris, but our core expertise remains the same. We focus on the wildlife and not the destinations. We focus on the right place and the right time in order to maximise your safari experience, not the brass taps on your bathtub. We are travel outfitters – butlers of the trade if you like - here to assist with your wildlife needs. We are not tour operators, we are not generalists, when it comes to wildlife, we are here to help.

Our Destination Specialists

We pride ourselves on our expertise throughout the office. Whilst it is not always possible to have visited every single lodge or park within each destination that we sell, we are almost there! The majority of our destination specialists have all lived or worked in the destinations that they specialise in, giving them an unparalleled insight into the workings of each individual destination, and more importantly, the wildlife within in. We have ex-guides, we have ex-lodge managers, we have ex-destination managers and we have ex-conservationists. All of this expertise forms an integral part of Natural World Safaris and the level of expertise that we like to offer our clients to ensure a truly unique journey into the extraordinary. 
Meet the Specialists

Conservation

We have been told we should sing and shout about this more – but we don’t want to. Whilst some of our competitors profess to be experts in this arena, we do not. Conservationists are best at conservation and travel outfitters are best at travel. What we try and do is do our best to blend the two together wherever and whenever possible.  We work with grass-root projects in each destination, to ensure that any money goes directly to those enterprises in need, rather than being lost in the world of ‘non-profits’.  Some of our key partners on the ground include: Gorilla Doctors in Rwanda, Kianjavato in Madagascar, TOFT in India, Ocean Heritage Trust in Sri Lanka, Sun Bear Conservation project in Borneo and more.
